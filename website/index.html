A Tale of Two Worlds

The Whisk meets the Bean

Matcha & Coffee was born out of a deep personal journey to solve a daily urban dilemma: the perpetual cycle of high caffeine anxiety. We loved the rich, full-bodied aromatic roast of morning specialty espresso, but we craved the calm, steady, and mindful visual focus of traditional stone-ground Japanese matcha.

Instead of forcing city dwellers to choose, we designed a minimalist sanctuary in 2024 that gracefully blends both beautiful craft heritages. From our traditional imported chasen bamboo whisks to our modern high-pressure lever espresso pull machines, we treat both drinks with absolute peak reverence.
